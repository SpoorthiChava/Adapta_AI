# Requirements Document: Adaptive Study Planner (Adapta)

## Introduction

Adapta is an AI-powered adaptive study planner designed to help students prepare for academic and competitive exams through personalized, continuously adapting study schedules. The system monitors student performance, engagement, and well-being to dynamically adjust workload and prevent burnout while maintaining learning consistency.

## Glossary

- **Adapta**: The adaptive study planner system
- **Study_Schedule**: A time-based plan of study tasks generated by the system
- **Task**: A discrete study activity with a subject, topic, duration, and deadline
- **Performance_Data**: Metrics including quiz scores, task completion rates, and accuracy
- **Engagement_Signal**: Indicators of student activity including login frequency, task interaction, and participation
- **Mood_Input**: Student-provided emotional state data used for workload adjustment
- **Workload**: The total amount of study tasks assigned within a time period
- **Replanning**: The process of regenerating or adjusting the study schedule based on new data
- **Exam_Mode**: Planning mode focused on preparing for specific academic or competitive exams
- **Skill_Mode**: Planning mode focused on skill-based learning goals without fixed exam deadlines
- **Learning_Mode**: Sub-mode within Exam_Mode focused on concept learning and understanding
- **Revision_Mode**: Sub-mode within Exam_Mode focused on quick review and reinforcement
- **Learning_Preference**: User-configured settings for AI teaching style and explanation format
- **Resource**: External learning material including videos, articles, and books
- **Syllabus_Database**: Repository of official syllabi for major competitive and academic exams
- **RAG**: Retrieval-Augmented Generation for quiz creation from study materials
- **Chatbot**: AI assistant for answering questions and providing explanations
- **Streak**: Consecutive days of task completion
- **Burnout_Signal**: Indicators suggesting student exhaustion including missed tasks, declining performance, or negative mood patterns

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Study Schedule Generation

**User Story:** As a student, I want the system to generate a personalized study schedule based on my syllabus and constraints, so that I have a clear plan to follow.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a student provides syllabus, exam deadline, subject difficulty ratings, and daily availability, THE Adapta SHALL generate a complete study schedule covering all topics
2. WHEN generating a schedule, THE Adapta SHALL distribute topics across available time slots while respecting daily availability constraints
3. WHEN multiple subjects are provided, THE Adapta SHALL prioritize tasks based on exam proximity, subject difficulty, and student-indicated weak areas
4. THE Adapta SHALL assign realistic task durations based on topic complexity and student proficiency level
5. WHEN in Exam_Mode, THE Adapta SHALL ensure all syllabus topics are covered before the exam deadline with buffer time for revision

### Requirement 2: Hierarchical Planning Modes

**User Story:** As a student, I want to choose between exam preparation and skill development modes with appropriate sub-modes, so that the planner adapts to my learning context and phase.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Adapta SHALL support both Exam_Mode and Skill_Mode as distinct top-level planning contexts
2. WHEN in Exam_Mode, THE Adapta SHALL provide access to Learning_Mode and Revision_Mode as sub-modes
3. WHEN in Skill_Mode, THE Adapta SHALL organize tasks around skill mastery goals without Learning_Mode or Revision_Mode options
4. WHEN in Learning_Mode, THE Adapta SHALL focus tasks on concept understanding and problem-solving practice
5. WHEN in Revision_Mode, THE Adapta SHALL focus tasks on quick review, formula recall, and high-yield concepts
6. WHEN switching between modes, THE Adapta SHALL preserve existing progress data and replan accordingly
7. THE Adapta SHALL prevent access to Learning_Mode and Revision_Mode when in Skill_Mode

### Requirement 3: Performance-Based Adaptation

**User Story:** As a student, I want the system to adjust my schedule based on how well I'm performing, so that I spend more time on topics I struggle with.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a student completes a quiz, THE Adapta SHALL record the score and topic-specific accuracy in Performance_Data
2. WHEN Performance_Data indicates low scores on specific topics, THE Adapta SHALL increase task allocation for those topics in future schedules
3. WHEN Performance_Data indicates high scores on specific topics, THE Adapta SHALL reduce task allocation for those topics and reallocate time to weaker areas
4. WHEN task completion rate falls below 70% over a 3-day period, THE Adapta SHALL trigger replanning to adjust workload

### Requirement 4: Engagement Monitoring and Burnout Prevention

**User Story:** As a student, I want the system to detect when I'm becoming disengaged or burned out, so that it can adjust my workload before I give up.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Adapta SHALL track Engagement_Signals including login frequency, task start times, task completion times, and days of inactivity
2. WHEN a student misses tasks for 2 consecutive days, THE Adapta SHALL mark this as a Burnout_Signal
3. WHEN Engagement_Signals show declining activity patterns, THE Adapta SHALL reduce daily workload by 20-30%
4. WHEN multiple Burnout_Signals are detected within a 7-day period, THE Adapta SHALL restructure the schedule to include more breaks and shorter study sessions
5. WHEN Engagement_Signals return to normal levels, THE Adapta SHALL gradually increase workload back to optimal levels

### Requirement 5: Mood-Based Workload Adjustment

**User Story:** As a student, I want to input how I'm feeling, so that the system can adjust my workload when I'm stressed or tired.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Adapta SHALL provide a daily mood input interface accepting values on a scale from 1 (very low) to 5 (very high)
2. WHEN a student inputs a mood value of 1 or 2, THE Adapta SHALL reduce that day's workload by 30-50%
3. WHEN a student inputs a mood value of 4 or 5, THE Adapta SHALL maintain or slightly increase workload within healthy limits
4. WHEN mood inputs show a declining trend over 3 consecutive days, THE Adapta SHALL trigger schedule restructuring
5. THE Adapta SHALL store mood history and correlate it with performance data for future planning

### Requirement 6: Dynamic Replanning

**User Story:** As a student, I want the system to automatically adjust my schedule when I fall behind or circumstances change, so that I don't have to manually reorganize everything.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a student misses scheduled tasks, THE Adapta SHALL redistribute those tasks across future available time slots
2. WHEN exam deadlines are modified, THE Adapta SHALL regenerate the schedule to accommodate the new timeline
3. WHEN daily availability changes, THE Adapta SHALL replan all future tasks to fit the new constraints
4. WHEN replanning occurs, THE Adapta SHALL preserve completed task history and adjust only pending tasks
5. THE Adapta SHALL notify the student when significant replanning occurs with a summary of changes

### Requirement 7: RAG-Based Quiz Generation

**User Story:** As a student, I want the system to generate quizzes from my study materials, so that I can test my understanding of topics.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a student uploads study materials for a topic, THE Adapta SHALL store them in a retrievable format
2. WHEN a student requests a quiz on a topic, THE Adapta SHALL use RAG to generate questions based on the uploaded materials
3. THE Adapta SHALL generate quizzes with multiple question types including multiple choice, short answer, and true/false
4. WHEN a quiz is completed, THE Adapta SHALL provide immediate feedback with correct answers and explanations
5. THE Adapta SHALL store quiz results in Performance_Data for adaptive planning

### Requirement 8: AI Chatbot for Learning Support

**User Story:** As a student, I want to ask questions and get explanations from an AI assistant, so that I can clarify doubts while studying.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Adapta SHALL provide a Chatbot interface accessible from any study task
2. WHEN a student asks a question, THE Chatbot SHALL provide explanations using RAG from uploaded study materials
3. WHEN study materials are insufficient, THE Chatbot SHALL provide general explanations with clear indication of source limitations
4. THE Chatbot SHALL support follow-up questions and maintain conversation context within a study session
5. THE Chatbot SHALL support multilingual interactions based on student language preference

### Requirement 9: Revision Notes Management

**User Story:** As a student, I want to create and organize revision notes, so that I can quickly review key concepts before exams.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Adapta SHALL provide a notes interface where students can create, edit, and organize revision notes by subject and topic
2. WHEN a student creates a note, THE Adapta SHALL associate it with the relevant subject and topic for easy retrieval
3. THE Adapta SHALL support rich text formatting including headings, lists, and highlighting in notes
4. THE Adapta SHALL allow students to search notes by keyword or topic
5. WHEN in revision phase, THE Adapta SHALL surface relevant notes alongside scheduled revision tasks

### Requirement 10: Streak Tracking and Insights

**User Story:** As a student, I want to see my study streaks and performance insights, so that I stay motivated and understand my progress.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Adapta SHALL track consecutive days of task completion as a Streak
2. WHEN a student completes at least one task per day, THE Adapta SHALL increment the Streak counter
3. WHEN a student misses all tasks for a day, THE Adapta SHALL reset the Streak to zero
4. THE Adapta SHALL display weekly and monthly insights including total study time, topics covered, average quiz scores, and completion rates
5. THE Adapta SHALL visualize performance trends over time with charts showing progress by subject

### Requirement 11: Email Notifications

**User Story:** As a student, I want to receive email reminders about my tasks and schedule changes, so that I stay on track even when not using the app.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Adapta SHALL send daily email summaries of scheduled tasks for the day
2. WHEN replanning occurs, THE Adapta SHALL send an email notification with a summary of schedule changes
3. WHEN a student's Streak reaches milestone numbers, THE Adapta SHALL send congratulatory emails
4. THE Adapta SHALL allow students to configure email notification preferences including frequency and types
5. WHEN a student shows Burnout_Signals, THE Adapta SHALL send supportive emails with workload adjustment information

### Requirement 12: Multilingual Support

**User Story:** As a student, I want to use the system in my preferred language, so that I can learn more comfortably.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Adapta SHALL support multiple interface languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Hindi, and Mandarin
2. WHEN a student selects a language preference, THE Adapta SHALL display all interface elements in that language
3. THE Chatbot SHALL respond in the student's selected language
4. WHEN generating quizzes, THE Adapta SHALL present questions in the student's selected language
5. THE Adapta SHALL allow language switching at any time without data loss

### Requirement 13: Accessibility Features

**User Story:** As a student with accessibility needs, I want the system to support keyboard navigation, simplified UI, voice input, and focus mode, so that I can use the planner effectively.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Adapta SHALL support full keyboard navigation for all interface elements with visible focus indicators
2. WHERE simplified UI mode is enabled, THE Adapta SHALL reduce visual complexity and use larger fonts and buttons
3. WHERE voice input is enabled, THE Adapta SHALL accept voice commands for task completion, mood input, and chatbot queries
4. WHERE focus mode is enabled, THE Adapta SHALL hide non-essential UI elements and display only the current task
5. THE Adapta SHALL comply with WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards for color contrast and screen reader compatibility

### Requirement 14: Data Persistence and Synchronization

**User Story:** As a student, I want my data to be saved and accessible across devices, so that I can study anywhere.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Adapta SHALL persist all student data including schedules, performance data, notes, and preferences
2. WHEN a student logs in from a different device, THE Adapta SHALL synchronize and display current data within 5 seconds
3. WHEN network connectivity is lost, THE Adapta SHALL allow offline access to previously loaded schedules and notes
4. WHEN connectivity is restored, THE Adapta SHALL synchronize any offline changes with the server
5. THE Adapta SHALL maintain data integrity during synchronization conflicts by preserving the most recent changes

### Requirement 15: Task Prioritization Algorithm

**User Story:** As a student, I want the system to intelligently prioritize my tasks, so that I focus on what matters most.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Adapta SHALL calculate task priority scores based on exam proximity, subject difficulty, performance data, and topic dependencies
2. WHEN displaying daily tasks, THE Adapta SHALL order them by priority score from highest to lowest
3. WHEN workload reduction is needed, THE Adapta SHALL defer lower-priority tasks first
4. THE Adapta SHALL ensure high-priority topics receive multiple review sessions before exam dates
5. WHEN a student consistently struggles with a topic, THE Adapta SHALL elevate its priority regardless of other factors

### Requirement 16: Smart Resource Aggregation

**User Story:** As a student, I want the system to automatically provide relevant learning resources for each task, so that I have quality materials to study from.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a task is generated, THE Adapta SHALL automatically fetch and attach curated learning resources including YouTube videos, articles, and search links
2. THE Adapta SHALL rank resources based on topic relevance, exam type, and student performance level
3. WHEN a student has performance history, THE Adapta SHALL prioritize resources that match their learning level
4. WHERE applicable, THE Adapta SHALL suggest recommended books for topics
5. THE Adapta SHALL track resource engagement and use this data to improve future resource recommendations
6. WHEN resources are fetched, THE Adapta SHALL provide at least 3 different resource types per task when available

### Requirement 17: Automatic Syllabus Sourcing

**User Story:** As a student preparing for a major exam, I want the system to automatically load the official syllabus, so that I don't have to manually enter all topics.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Adapta SHALL maintain a Syllabus_Database containing official syllabi for major exams including JEE, NEET, UPSC, GATE, SAT, and GRE
2. WHEN a student selects a recognized exam from the database, THE Adapta SHALL automatically load the complete syllabus with subjects and topics
3. WHEN a syllabus is auto-loaded, THE Adapta SHALL tag each topic with subject classification and difficulty level
4. WHEN a syllabus is loaded, THE Adapta SHALL allow students to customize by adding, removing, or modifying topics
5. THE Adapta SHALL support annual syllabus updates and notify students when updated syllabi are available for their selected exam
6. WHEN an exam is not in the database, THE Adapta SHALL allow manual syllabus entry

### Requirement 18: Personalized AI Learning Preferences

**User Story:** As a student, I want to customize how the AI explains concepts and provides revision content, so that I learn in the way that works best for me.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN in Learning_Mode, THE Adapta SHALL allow students to configure learning preferences including explanation depth, problem-solving style, language formality, example preference, and analogy usage
2. WHEN in Revision_Mode, THE Adapta SHALL allow students to configure revision preferences including summary format, bullet-point density, formula-only option, and high-yield focus
3. THE Adapta SHALL store all Learning_Preference settings in the student profile
4. WHEN a student modifies preferences, THE Adapta SHALL apply changes immediately to subsequent AI interactions
5. THE Adapta SHALL allow students to edit preferences at any time without data loss
6. WHEN the Chatbot generates explanations, THE Adapta SHALL inject the student's learning preferences into the system prompt
7. WHEN generating revision content, THE Adapta SHALL inject the student's revision preferences into the system prompt

### Requirement 19: Prompt Management System

**User Story:** As a system, I need to dynamically construct AI prompts based on user preferences and context, so that responses are personalized and relevant.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Adapta SHALL implement a Prompt Management Layer that combines base system prompts with user preferences
2. WHEN generating AI responses, THE Adapta SHALL construct prompts by combining base prompt, Learning_Preference settings, topic context, and RAG-retrieved knowledge
3. THE Adapta SHALL maintain separate base prompts for Learning_Mode and Revision_Mode
4. WHEN user preferences change, THE Adapta SHALL update the prompt construction without requiring system restart
5. THE Adapta SHALL ensure prompt construction completes within 100ms to maintain response latency
